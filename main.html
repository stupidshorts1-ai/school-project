The 12 Jyotirlingas of Mahadev

हर हर महादेव!

Number Devasthana God City District
1. Shri Kshetra Dharmasthala Manjunatha Swamy Devasthana
2. Shri Chamundeshwari Devi Devasthana
3. Shri Shrikanteshwara Swamy Devasthana and Dakshina Kashi Nanjangudu
4. Virupaksha Devasthana, Hampi
5. Shri Shakambhari Shakti Peetham
6. Ancient Hoysala Shri Hoysaleswara Devasthana
7. Shri Kalabhairaveshwara Swamy Devasthana
8. Kolluru Shri Mookambika Devi Devasthana
9. Shri Sharada Peeta
10. Udupi Shri Krishna Matha
11. Shri Murudeshwara Shiva Devasthana
12. Shri Mahabaleshwara Devasthana
13. Shrirangapatna - both Ranganathaswamy Devasthana and Nimishambha
14. Shri Horanadu Annapoorneshwari Devasthana
15. Hasanamba Devasthana

नमः पार्वती पतये - हर हर महादेव!

*Locations of some Jyotirlingas are disputed as they are not mentioned clearly in the Shiv Puran. The locations shown here are the most widely accepted ones, with others on the site pages.
